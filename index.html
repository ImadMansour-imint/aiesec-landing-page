Develop your leadership with aiesec

AIESEC is a global platform for young people to develop their leadership potential through international internships and volunteer opportunities. Founded in 1948, AIESEC is a non-governmental, and not-for-profit organization entirely run by youth for youth

  • BECOME A VOLUNTEER

    Find the right job through high quality listings and search for roles based on title, technology stack, salary, location, and more.

  • BECOME AN INTERN

    Develop your leadership while boosting your career prospects with an international internship in your field

  • BECOME A TEACHER

    Develop your leadership while boosting your career prospects with an opportunity to work as a teacher abroad
